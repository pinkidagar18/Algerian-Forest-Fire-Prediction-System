Understanding FWI Predictions

The Fire Weather Index (FWI) is a numerical rating system that indicates the potential intensity of a forest fire. Our machine learning model analyzes weather conditions and fuel moisture codes to calculate risk levels. Higher FWI values represent greater fire danger.
